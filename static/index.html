Fraud rate by hour of day
Fraud peaks sharply at 7am (10.6%). Fraudsters exploit early-morning windows when automated monitoring is lowest and transaction volume is minimal.
Fraud rate by card network
Discover cards show the highest fraud rate (7.7%). American Express shows the lowest (2.9%) reflecting stronger issuer-side controls.
Fraud rate by email domain — top 10
mail.com shows an 18.9% fraud rate, nearly 5x the overall average. Domains like outlook.es and aim.com appear disproportionately in fraud transactions.
Transaction amount distribution
Fraud transactions (red) concentrate at specific price points under $200. Legitimate transactions (green) show a wider spread.
Card age at time of transaction
Fraud transactions (red) concentrate heavily at day 0. Stolen cards are used immediately. Cards under 30 days old are 4x more likely to be fraudulent.
